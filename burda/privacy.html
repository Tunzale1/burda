Privacy Policy for Burda


Last Updated: April 13th, 2024

Thank you for using Burda. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Burda, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy.


1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information from you when you use our app:

Name and Surname: For identification purposes and to personalize your experience.
Email: To communicate with you and send updates, offers, and promotional content (if you opt-in).
Phone Number: For communication purposes and to provide support.
Gender: To personalize your experience within the app.
Location Information: To offer location-based services and personalized content. We may collect location data continuously or only while the app is being used, depending on your app settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information collected from you for the following purposes: ‍

Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our app services.
Personalization: To customize your app experience based on your preferences and usage.
Communication: To send you important updates, marketing communications, and customer support.
Legal Compliance: To fulfill legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests from law enforcement agencies.
App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance our app based on user feedback. ‍

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We may share your personal information under the following circumstances:

‍Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app and require access to your information for that purpose. These parties must adhere to confidentiality and data protection obligations.
Legal Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.
Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred to the new entity.
Your Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes. ‍

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

‍ 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

‍ 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Correction: You may request access to and correct the personal information we hold about you.
Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
Objection and Restriction: You may object to certain processing of your personal information and request a restriction on processing.
Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal information in a portable format.
Withdrawing Consent: If we process your data based on consent, you may withdraw your consent at any time.To exercise these rights, please contact us at hi@burda.social.

7. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

‍ 8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately. ‍

9. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any changes by updating the policy on our app and indicating the last updated date. ‍

10. Contact Us If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this privacy policy or how your personal information is handled, please contact us at:

Email: hi@burda.social
Address: Baku, Azerbaijan ‍

Please review this policy carefully and consult legal counsel if needed to ensure your privacy policy complies with all relevant laws and regulations.
